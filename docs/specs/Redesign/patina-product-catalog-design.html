01 — Overview

Three tiers, one catalog.

The Patina product catalog isn't a flat database — it's organized around The Curated Shelf: three tiers that reflect the reality of how designers furnish complete rooms. Every product carries not just specs and pricing, but living intelligence from The Aesthete Engine.

★ Maker Piece
Patina Partners

Handcrafted anchor pieces from makers we know personally. Full provenance, maker stories, and heritage documentation. These are the pieces clients will build rooms around.

✓ Designer's Pick
Designer Picks

Sofas, rugs, and lighting chosen by designers from brands they trust. Quality-verified and style-classified but not artisan-exclusive. The supporting cast that completes a room.

○ Sourced
Sourced Selections

Paint colors, hardware, throw pillows — the finishing details. Designer-approved, sourced from trusted retailers. The touches that make a room feel complete.

Core Principle — Leah's Time Creates Compounding Value

Every screen follows three rules.

The product is always the hero — images first, specs second, intelligence third. No page loads without showing what the product looks like within the first viewport.

Filters are visible text, not hidden menus. A designer scanning 800 products needs to narrow results with two clicks, not four. Tier badges, style tags, and category pills all filter inline.

Teaching is embedded, not separate. The Aesthete intelligence panel appears directly on the product detail page, not behind a different portal login. Browse and teach happen in the same flow.

The form is a document. Product editing uses the same vertical scroll with strata dividers — identity, specs, pricing, media — not tabs that hide information. Everything visible in one continuous read.
