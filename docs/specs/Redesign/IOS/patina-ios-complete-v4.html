Core Innovation

The Companion

Five static tabs ask "where can I go?" The Companion asks "what should I do next?" Built from the Strata Mark — three lines of decreasing width that are the DNA of Patina's brand identity — it floats at the bottom of every screen, breathing. It's not navigation. It's guidance.

Interaction Patterns

Gestures, Edges & Accessibility

Gestures

Swipe Vocabulary

Right = Save. Left = Not for me (trains Aesthete Engine). Up = Share. Hold = Material close-up. All gestures have tap alternatives for accessibility.

Haptics

Tactile Feedback

Gentle tap on wall recognition. Soft impact on AR floor snap. Spring bounce on save. No haptic on negative actions — silence is intentional.

Transitions

Movement Language

Cards cascade from vanishing point. Quiz questions cross-dissolve with vertical offset. AR furniture fades 60%→100%. Spring physics throughout — never linear easing.

Loading

Strata Animation

Three lines draw left to right with staggered timing. "Taking a moment to curate..." replaces spinners. Progress is organic, like tea steeping.

Edge Cases

Warm Recovery

Poor light: "Let's brighten things up." Complex room: "Large space! Let's focus on one area." Network fail: "Taking a moment to curate..." Empty room: "A blank canvas — exciting!"

Accessibility

WCAG AA+

44pt minimum touch targets. VoiceOver labels on all elements. Reduced motion mode. High contrast maintaining brand warmth. Text scaling 100-150%. AR audio cues for guidance.

Five tabs became one mark.

Twenty-eight screens. Five Companion states. Zero tab bars. The Strata Mark — three lines of decreasing width — carries the entire navigation of the application. It breathes when resting, nudges when you need direction, expands when you ask, tracks your journey during scanning, and whispers in the corner during AR.

And when you sit down at your laptop and visit patina.cloud, the same mark that guided your style discovery becomes the key that unlocks the door. Scan, confirm, connected. No password. No friction. Your phone holds your identity. The web just asks.

The type does the hierarchy. The whitespace builds the rooms. The Companion guides the journey. And the interface becomes invisible — letting you focus on what matters: creating a space that tells your story.
