The Companion

A complete reimagination of Patina's iOS navigation. The bottom tab bar is gone. In its place: a single, living element — The Companion — built from the Strata Mark itself. It breathes. It knows where you are. It suggests what's next. And it holds the key that connects your phone to every Patina portal.

Core Concept

The Strata Mark, alive.

Five static tabs treat navigation as a wayfinding problem — "where can I go?" The Companion treats it as a guidance problem — "what should I do next?" It's the difference between a building directory and a knowledgeable friend walking beside you.

Authentication Architecture

The QR Handshake

A four-step flow that eliminates passwords entirely for web portals. The phone is the authenticator. The web is the display.

Step 1

Portal Displays Code

User visits patina.cloud/designer (or /client, /admin). Instead of a login form, the page displays a QR code with a session token. The code refreshes every 60 seconds. Copy reads: "Scan with your Patina app to sign in."

Step 2

User Opens Scanner

User taps The Companion → "Connect to portal." Camera opens in QR mode with the Clay-cornered reticle. The scan line sweeps. DM Mono instruction text reads: "Point at the code on your screen."

Step 3

Phone Confirms

QR decoded. Phone shows which portal is requesting access (Designer Portal, patina.cloud/designer) and asks for confirmation. Single tap "Confirm" sends the auth token. Biometric (Face ID / Touch ID) optionally gates the confirmation.

Step 4

Portal Unlocks

Web portal receives the authenticated session via WebSocket. Page transitions from QR code to the logged-in dashboard with a smooth fade. Phone shows the success screen with a check mark and "Connected" confirmation.

One mark. Every context.

The Companion is the Strata Mark made alive. It breathes at the bottom of every screen — three lines that carry the entire brand. Tap it, and it reveals exactly what you need based on where you are. During a scan, it becomes your progress tracker. In AR, it shrinks to a whisper. On the home screen, it nudges you to scan a room. On your profile, it suggests talking to a designer.

And when you sit down at your laptop and visit patina.cloud, the same Companion that's been guiding your style journey becomes the key that unlocks the door. No password. No email verification. Just scan, confirm, connected. Your phone holds your identity. The web just needs to ask.

Five tab bars became one living mark. That's The Companion. That's Patina.
